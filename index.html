February 13 — 9:32 p.m. A witness is in his living room at Ada, Oklahoma, when he notices that his son has driven onto his property, but his car has stopped near the gate about 300 feet east of the house. He sits there about 10 minutes before coming inside and tells his father to come outside and look at two objects, one diamond-shaped some 300 feet above the ground and a doughnut-shaped object about 150 feet up and 100 feet west of the diamond. Both are a dull-orange color, silent, and about 900 feet away. After 10 minutes, the larger diamond-shaped object (about the size of a Boeing 727) begins moving slowly to the east, passing behind some trees before it rises to an elevation of about 700–800 feet, hovering for another 10 minutes. It then moves east and climbs to 1,000 feet, then moves north to 1,500 feet and begins flashing. It moves north and disappears. The doughnut-shaped object is still stationary and seems to fade away. (Roger Marsh, “ Oklahoma Witnesses Describe Two UFOs near Ground Level,” Open Minds, February 20, 2015)
